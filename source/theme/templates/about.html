About Matt Makai

I am currently traveling to thirty cities around the United States in five months. I'm talking to tech startups, accelerators, and entrepreneurs to tell the stories of people using software to solve interesting problems. My project is self-funded and is called Coding Across America. Let's tell your story. Reach out to me on Twitter or at my email shown below.

I work remotely for Excella Consulting, an Arlington, Virginia-based tech consultancy building web applications for commercial, non-profit, and government clients.

My primarily technology skill set is in the Python ecosystem. My secondary programming capabilities are in the JVM stack with Java and Clojure. I also create actionable data visualizations, primarily with d3.js and I'm beginning to get into data analysis with pandas, numpy, scipy, matplotlib, and iPython.
